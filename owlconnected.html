OWL Connected E-Magazine

Role:
UI/UX Design, Visual Design Interactivity

Tools:
InDesign, Photoshop, Aquafadas

Project:
In 2017 Owlkids decided to produce their first ever digital e-magazine, and I had an opportunity to be apart of the design team. It currently publishes bi-weekly and features a plethora of engaging stories for kids 9-13. Each issue covers various topics in pop culture, sports, STEAM, and entertainment.

(Application user flow)

Our goal is to provide an easy to use application for the user. We are able to provide a guided navigational journey throughout the entire issue, while still allowing for freedom if the user decides to skip certain sections.

(Navigation UI kit)

Bold navigation buttons and signs for gestures are universal accross every issue.
